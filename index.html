About AndroShell

AndroShell is a versatile Android app that empowers Android pentesters to interact with their device using a custom terminal interface. With AndroShell, you can execute commands, perform system and root-level operations. It gives you the power of ADB Shell within the app.

Requirements

Device needs to be Rooted

Minimum Required Android Version

Android 4.1

Minimum Android 8.1 is required for optimum performance
